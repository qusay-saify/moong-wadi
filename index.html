How It's Made

From Dal to Delight

💧

Soaking the Moong Dal

Whole moong or split moong dal is washed thoroughly and soaked overnight in fresh water to soften and activate the nutrients.

⏱ 8–12 hours
🫙

Grinding the Batter

The soaked dal is coarsely ground — not too fine, not too rough — to get a thick, textured batter that binds well and dries evenly.

⏱ 20–30 minutes
🌶

Mixing in the Spices

Just three pure ingredients — fresh green chilli, garlic, and salt — are mixed into the batter. Masala Wadi gets more of these same spices for a bolder, punchier flavour.

⏱ 15 minutes
☀️

Sun Drying

Small dumplings are dropped onto cotton cloth and left under the open sun for 2–3 days. The heat naturally dries and firms them, giving that perfect chewy texture when cooked.

⏱ 2–3 days
📦

Pack & Dispatch

Fully dried wadi is packed in airtight bags. Local Ujjain orders are delivered fresh. Pan-India orders are couriered with secure packaging.

⏱ Ready to ship
